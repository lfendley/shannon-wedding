Venue

The San Roque Club

With its stunning location amidst lush greenery and breathtaking views of the Mediterranean Sea, the San Roque Club provides an idyllic setting for both ceremonies and receptions. Couples can choose from various elegant indoor and outdoor spaces, including manicured gardens, terraces, and banquet halls, all designed to accommodate weddings of different sizes and styles.

The venue's dedicated team of wedding specialists is committed to turning your vision into reality, offering personalized assistance every step of the way. From customized décor and floral arrangements to gourmet catering options featuring delectable Spanish cuisine, every detail is meticulously planned to exceed your expectations.

For those seeking relaxation and indulgence, the San Roque Club also boasts world-class amenities such as a championship golf course, luxury spa facilities, and upscale accommodations for you and your guests to enjoy throughout the celebration.
